FAQ

Common questions about AI

How much does an AI integration cost? expand_more
A simple LLM API integration starts from 999 PLN. A RAG chatbot trained on your data — from 3,999 PLN. Process automation and more complex AI systems are priced individually. Free quotes within 24h.
What is RAG and what does it do? expand_more
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) allows a language model to answer questions based on your own documents, knowledge base or website content. The result is a chatbot that genuinely knows your business — and doesn't hallucinate answers.
Is my data safe when using AI? expand_more
Yes. We can deploy AI in your private infrastructure (on-premise or private cloud) so no data ever leaves your servers. For cloud-based APIs, we use providers with strong data processing agreements (DPA).
Will AI replace my employees? expand_more
Not replace — augment. AI takes over repetitive, time-consuming tasks so your team can focus on work that genuinely requires a human. Most of our clients report saving 5–15 hours per week per person after implementation.
How long does AI implementation take? expand_more
A simple API integration — 1–2 weeks. A RAG chatbot — 2–4 weeks. Full business process automation — 4–8 weeks depending on the number of systems to be integrated.
