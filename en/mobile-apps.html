FAQ

Common questions about mobile apps

How much does a mobile app cost? expand_more
A simple cross-platform app starts from 2,999 PLN. Apps with user accounts and a backend — from 7,999 PLN. Complex platforms (marketplace, SaaS) are quoted individually. Quotes are always free.
How long does it take to build an app? expand_more
A simple app is delivered in 3–4 weeks. Apps with a backend and login — 6–10 weeks. More complex projects — we establish the timeline individually.
Will the app work on both iOS and Android? expand_more
Yes — we use React Native, which lets us build one app that runs natively on both platforms. You save time and cost compared to building two separate apps.
Do you help with App Store and Google Play publishing? expand_more
Yes. We handle the full process: developer account setup, preparing store descriptions and graphics, submitting the app and monitoring the review process.
Can you expand the app after it's launched? expand_more
Yes. Most of our clients work with us long-term — we add new features, publish updates and act on store reviews. We can also take over an existing React Native app.
