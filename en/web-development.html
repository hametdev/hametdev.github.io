FAQ

Common questions about web development

How much does a website cost? expand_more
A simple business landing page with full SEO starts from 799 PLN. CMS-powered websites start from 1,999 PLN. SEO audit and optimisation for existing sites — from 499 PLN. Web applications are priced individually. Quotes are always free and take up to 24 hours.
How long does it take to build a website? expand_more
A simple landing page with SEO can be delivered in 3–7 business days. CMS websites with multiple pages take 2–4 weeks. Complex web applications — we establish the timeline individually. SEO audit with report and implementation — typically up to 5 business days.
What does on-page SEO include exactly? expand_more
On-page SEO covers: proper heading structure (H1–H3), meta titles and descriptions, image alt attributes, Schema.org structured data, Core Web Vitals performance optimisation, clean URLs, sitemap.xml and robots.txt. Every site we build has all of this configured from the start.
How quickly will my site appear in Google after launch? expand_more
After launch we submit the site to Google Search Console and send the sitemap.xml. First indexing typically happens within 1–7 days. The site is technically ready for indexing from day one — visibility in results grows gradually.
Can I edit the website myself after launch? expand_more
Absolutely. On request we'll integrate a simple CMS panel so you can update text, images and blog posts yourself — no coding knowledge required.
