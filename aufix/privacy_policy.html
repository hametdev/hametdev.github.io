Last updated: January 27, 2026

Aufix ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Aufix. We demand the highest standards for data privacy and security.

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application, Aufix. By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information & Usage

We collect only the minimum amount of information necessary to provide the App's functionality:

  • Account Data: We collect your Email Address solely for authentication (logging in) and password recovery.
  • User Content: We store the expense records, car profiles, and vehicle data you input to sync them across your devices.
  • Photos: Car icons and user avatars that you choose to upload.

B. Device Permissions

The App may request access to specific features on your device. You can grant or deny these permissions at any time:

  • Camera & Photo Library: This permission is required strictly to allow you to take a photo of your vehicle for the car icon or to set a user profile picture. We do not use the camera for any other purpose.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our app functionality.
  • Create and sync your account and expenses across devices.
  • Authenticate your identity securely.
  • Manage your subscription status (Premium features).
  • Analyze anonymous usage patterns to improve user experience.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

We offer paid subscriptions for Premium features.

  • No Payment Data Stored: We do not collect or store your credit card information or billing details. All financial transactions are handled exclusively by Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  • Subscription Management: We use RevenueCat to validate your subscription status. We share only an anonymous App User ID with RevenueCat to check if you have an active "Pro" plan.

4. Third-Party Services

We may employ trusted third-party companies solely to help us operate the App:

Google Firebase: We use Firebase for secure authentication (handling passwords securely) and cloud database storage. Google Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat: Used for processing subscription logic and validating receipts from Apple/Google.

5. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize keeping your personal usage data on your device whenever possible for speed and privacy. However, essential data required for synchronization is stored in a secure cloud database (Google Firebase).

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

7. Deletion of Data

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

  • How to delete: You can find the "Delete Account" option directly inside the App settings.
  • Result: Upon confirmation, your account, email, expenses, and images are permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.
